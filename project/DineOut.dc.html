01

Overall

DineOut is an application where you can book a table at a selected restaurant. Your reservation will be confirmed and a record will be created in the system. Reservation can be mandatory, paid (for the fact of reservation itself), or with a deposit (for example, a deposit for each person), which is spent on paying for dishes on the menu. When you visit a restaurant, you open an account in the app, which is processed as orders are completed.

02

The problem and the task

Waiters spend too much time accepting payments from customers. The task is to make it possible to pay together through the application and to make it possible for customers to choose seats arrangement through the app.

03

Competitive analysis

I examined several products to gain insights into the market. This study revealed competitors’ advantages and flaws, enabling me to differentiate myself and avoid their mistakes.

06

Splitting the bill

There are three ways to split the bill:

  1. 01Evenly. The bill is being splitted among the users evenly, each participant pays the same amount.
  2. 02By percentages. Host is choosing percentage of payment for each user by his own.
  3. 03Separately. In this case, host can choose separate dish for separate person so users can pay only for the dishes they ordered.
