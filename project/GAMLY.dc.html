01

Overall

Gamly is an online store of digital goods. You can use it to top up Steam or other services. Payment is made through the internal currency Gamly Coin. The user may not have enough Gamly Coin to make a purchase.

02

The task

The task is to design a flow of the user’s interaction with the service after clicking the “refund Steam” button.

03

References

To begin with, I carefully studied the analogues to better understand the trends in this area.
